"Me, you, us" • Photo workshop 15/18 years old

This internship proposes to explore the notions of sharing and exchange, in the continuity of the theme chosen by the House of gesture and image in 2021-2022, "fraternity · sorority". From different photographic approaches – landscape, portrait, collage, web image research – and writing – words, poetry, texts, etc. -, we will create postcards to share (or send) to the internal or external public at the Mgi . The object created will be the means of transmission of these values which will be conveyed by the image, the word, as a "gift" to others. A metaphor of the social bond which requires fraternity, cement to build the world of tomorrow. We will transform them into large formats and exhibit them at the Mgi to leave a trace of our experience. All moments of sharing the image will be documented with photography and video.
